Featured Work: "Searching"

"Digital design always solves a problem otherwise it would be pixel art"

- Wouter de Bres

Why I started this project

Before implementing this piece the problem/need I had was that I was absolutely clueless when it came to graphics.

I saw this lack of knowledge as a problem for 2 major reasons:

    1. As a designer, I believe that it is important to able to connect with my production team
    2. Being able to draw whats on my mind is a useful skill for communication and wire-framing.

My Target Audience

The primary function for this particular piece was to make a Facebook cover photo. Therefore, the audience I had when creating this were my friends on Facebook. This would be a an age group mostly composed of 18-23 year-old college students. With this in mind, I tried to make a deeper theme (literally lol) while still maintaining a childish style.

Tools That Helped Me

Lots of online tutorials and critique from friends! Before starting this project I did a ton of tutorials to get a grasp of all the technical aspects that went into graphic art, until eventually I felt ready to create my own work.

One of my sketch books (for ideation phase).

The Result

The end result, a cover photo that represented me. Using flat and soothing colors it works well as a cover photo on Facebook serving the purpose of adding a little more character to my profile.

More Drawings!

Although I know graphic design is only one aspect of UX, I began to pick up the hobby of illustration. It was a spur of the moment decision to purchase adobe creative cloud but I figured it would be a good way to practice design elements. Drawing has been a good creative outlet for me and helps me understand the many rules of design.

The works displayed here are all clickable modals. These mostly compose of personal projects, ads my local church wanted me to make, and character designs. Hope you enjoy!
