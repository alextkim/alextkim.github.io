"Digital design always solves a problem otherwise it would be pixel art"

- Wouter de Bres

The Problem

Although this particular piece can be seen as pixel art when it stands alone, the practice of creating this along with other works solved a problem of mine. Before implementing this piece, the problem I had was that I was absolutely clueless when it came to graphics.

This was a problem for 2 major reasons:

    1. As a designer, I believe that it is important to able to connect with my production team
    2. Being able to draw whats on my mind is a useful skill for communication and wire-framing.

Tools That Helped Me

Lots of online tutorials and critique from friends! Before starting this project I did a ton of tutorials to get a grasp of all the technical aspects that went into graphic art, until eventually I felt ready to create my own work.

One of my sketch books (for ideation phase).

The Result

In the end, I was able to create my own cover photo that represented me from scratch! And I got to learn and understand the process of what a future art production team member goes through.
